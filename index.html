About

I started cake decorating when I was 16, helping my Mom decorate our Christmas cake every year.

When family and friends saw the cakes they started asking me to make them cakes of their own, it was then I discovered my love and talent for cake making.

In 2013 I began studying Baking & Pastry Arts in DIT, to develop my skills in a more formal setting.

I spent a few years working in bakeries, building my skills and knowledge.

I pride myself in creating beautiful cakes suited exactly to your requirements, for any occasion, using only the best quality ingredients.

Why not get in touch via the contact form below?

- Bronagh

Frequently Asked Questions

Q) How much do the cakes cost?

There is are no set prices on cakes outside of seasonal offerings (ie. Christmas, Valentine's Day), as the price varies greatly from cake to cake depending on ingredients, people to feed and detail. Consultations allow me to offer a variety of options catered to your wants and budget. Currently, my Valentine's offering is available. 6 cupcakes for €12, 12 cupcakes for €20 and share boxes for €25.

How do I place an order?

Simply use the "Contact" section of the website to send me an email and I will contact you to discuss your ideas. While seasonal offerings are on you can use this feature to book directly. On both occasions please be mindful of the "Calendar" feature on the website. When it indicates I am "fully booked", I am truly fully booked and cannot facilitate an order at this time.

How far in advance should I book?

I would recommend contacting me no later than one month in advance for smaller cakes, as there is limited availability and spaces tend to fill up fast. For larger cakes, I would recommend contacting me as soon as possible so that I can start work on your design and book you in to avoid disappointment.

Is delivery available?

Delivery is available on a case by case basis, if I can facilitate it I can deliver straight to your door. I am a one woman operation so occasionally I may not have the time, but where I can I will provide delivery to your door/venue.
