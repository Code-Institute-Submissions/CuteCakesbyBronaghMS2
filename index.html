About

I started cake decorating when I was 16, helping my Mom decorate our Christmas cake every year.

When family and friends saw the cakes they started asking me to make them cakes of their own, it was then I discovered my love and talent for cake making.

In 2013 I began studying Baking & Pastry Arts in DIT, to develop my skills in a more formal setting.

I spent a few years working in bakeries, building my skills and knowledge.

I pride myself in creating beautiful cakes suited exactly to your requirements, for any occasion, using only the best quality ingredients.

Why not get in touch via the contact form below?

- Bronagh
